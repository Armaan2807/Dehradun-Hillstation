Nag Tibba Trek – Eye Witness The Magnificent Hills

Nag Tibba, literally meaning the Serpent’s Peak, is a part of the 3 ranges of the Lesser Himalayas – Dhauladhar range, Pir Panjal range, and Nag Tibba range. Located at an elevation of 3050 m above the sea level, Nag Tibba is the highest peak in the lower Himalayas of the Garhwal region. To enjoy the clear views of Bandarpunch peak, Kedarnath peak, and Chanabang peaks, all one has to do is take the exciting Nag Tibba treks in Dehradun.

Deoban trek

Deoban is legitimately a very peaceful, unruffled and unflustered place which is located near Chakrata and is the best alternative for weekend gateways. According to a folk take, Deoban means meadows of Deodar trees, and the locale absolutely has the predominance of them. The summit bestows tremendously admirable views of the mountain regions.

Rishikesh Trek – Get Magnetize By Amazing Forests

The forests in Rishikesh have exciting trails that lead travelers to the hidden waterfalls shrouded in the embrace of Rajaji National Park. If you are someone who loves to hike often, then a waterfall trekking in Rishikesh is just the perfect exercise to pump you up. Some of the popular waterfalls in the national park include Neer Garh Waterfall Trek, Garud Chatti, and Phool Chatti Waterfall Trek.

Dhanaulti Trek – Allure The Greenery

With more and more people looking to break free from their busy city lives and enjoy the gifts of nature, holidays in the hills are fast gaining popularity. The scenic views of the Garhwal Himalayan range have made trekking in Dhanaulti an alluring experience. Thanks to its elevation of 2,286 m above the sea level, Dhanaulti serves as one of the best places to visit in Uttarakhand to enjoy camping

Chakrata Trek – Sense The Serene Environment

Upon your arrival at Chakrata in the morning, the mountain beauty would leave you stunned and enchanted for sure. Located right in nature’s cozy comfort, Chakrata is just the perfect place to take away all your worries. Camping tents are available at a nominal cost which can be fixed at the sprawling campsite ground.
