The Tibetan Market

If you’re visiting Dehradun, your trip will be incomplete without a visit to this vibrant and lively market. Here you can find a range of reasonably-priced artefacts, jewellery, trinkets and prayer flags. Some of the items you must check out here include the Tibetan handcrafts and Garhwali jewellery. The items you get here make for great return presents to take home.

Pacific Mall for High-Street Fashion Brand

This is one of the most popular malls in Dehradun and offers a variety of shopping options. Here you will find a number of high street brands (Levis, Celio) as well as Indian ones (Max and Bata). There is also a huge food court with all the fast food joints as well as Movenpick, a popular brand for gourmet ice cream. You can catch the latest movies here as it has PVR screens. It’s a popular hangout spot for locals as well as visitors.

English Book Depot for Books

It is one of the best places to visit if you love getting lost in literature. Some of the finds here include a large selection of fiction, non-fiction, travelogues, magazines, journals, and newspapers. This shop is a perfect spot for bookworms to spend an entire day and is co-located with a coffee shop, which makes it a great spot in Dehradun to unwind. This is a must-visit place in Dehradun.

Crossroads Mall for Luxury Items

This is one of the larger malls in Dehradun and is a shopper’s paradise with over 50 branded stores. Here you can find labels such as Westside, Apple Store, Body Shop, Caterpillar, Puma, Adidas, Benetton, Levis, Wrangler, Mufti, Arrow, Flying Machine and more. Apart from being a shopping hub, it also offers a lot of food options and entertainment with events being hosted every weekend. It’s a fun place for the full family.
